About Dwight Tech Solution

Founded in 2020 by a group of ambitious innovators and students from Limkokwing University, Dwight Tech Solution is a technology company dedicated to providing cutting-edge software and multimedia solutions that empower businesses and organizations to thrive in a rapidly evolving digital world.

Our founders — John Tholley, Mohamed G. Kamara, and Abu Bakarr Shadrach Kamara — are passionate software engineering and multimedia students in their fourth semester, second-year students. Together, they combined their technical expertise, creativity, and drive for excellence to establish a company that bridges the gap between technology and everyday business needs.

Our Story

The inception of Dwight Tech Solution was born from a shared vision: to transform how businesses leverage technology for growth and innovation. As university students, we recognized a growing demand for personalized, innovative, and reliable tech solutions — but also saw a gap in quality, affordability, and creative integration.

Starting from humble beginnings, our journey has been fueled by hard work, collaboration, and an unyielding commitment to quality. From academic projects to real-world challenges, we consistently push boundaries, refining our skills and expanding our expertise to meet and exceed client expectations.

Our Vision

We envision Dwight Tech Solution as a leading technology partner known for delivering innovative, sustainable, and scalable solutions tailored to the unique challenges faced by modern businesses and institutions. Our goal is to empower our clients through technology, enabling them to operate more efficiently, creatively, and competitively in their respective industries.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide top-tier software development, technical support, and creative multimedia services that are accessible, reliable, and customer-focused. We strive to create a positive impact through technology by:

  • Listening carefully to understand client needs
  • Designing customized solutions that align with business goals
  • Emphasizing user experience and seamless functionality
  • Delivering projects on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards

What Makes Us Unique

At Dwight Tech Solution, we combine technical proficiency with creative insight. Our multidisciplinary background in software engineering and multimedia enables us to craft solutions that are not only powerful and efficient but also visually engaging and user-friendly.

We are proud to be young innovators who bring fresh perspectives, modern tools, and an energetic approach to every project. We value transparency, integrity, and collaboration — working closely with clients as partners, not just vendors.
